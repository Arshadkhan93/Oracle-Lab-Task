2.Write a query to add a new column email into STUDENTS table.
ANS: SQL> ALTER TABLE STUDENTS ADD EMAIL VARCHAR2(20);

ANS: SQL> ALTER TABLE STUDENTS MODIFY SNAME VARCHAR2(20);
